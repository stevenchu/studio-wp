ABOUT STEVEN CHU

Steven is a digital product designer (hybrid designer + developer), instructor, and photographer based in NYC. With 12+ years experience, Steven started as an mobile marketing tour experience designer for Jack Morton Worldwide, moving into advertising as digital art director at FCB & Deutsch, and senior art director at Ketchum Change. Steven is also a long-time photographer and brand consultant for fashion brands. He currently toggles his time between socially driven causes, creative collaborations, and designing great user experiences.
